Types of tastes as per Ayurveda.


DESCRIPTION

SIX TYPES OF TASTES ARE DESCRIBED IN AYURVEDA
Taste (Ras) Elements Dosha Description
Sweet Earth + Water Increases Kapha, Decreases Vata and Pitta. (Increases Weight)
Sour Earth + Fire Increases Kapha and Pitta, Decreases Vata. (Increases Weight)
Salt Water + Fire Increases Kapha and Pitta, Decreases Vata. (Increases Weight)
Pungent Air + Fire Increases Vata and Pitta. Decreases Kapha. (Decreases Weight)
Astringent Earth + Air Increases Vata. Decreases Kapha. (Decreases Weight)
Bitter Air + Space Increases Vata. Decreases Kapha and Pitta. (Decreases Weight)

SWEET
Nourishes body, builds muscles.
Calms the sense organs.
Good for eyes and brain. Improves memory power.
Cool (Sheeta), Increases weight (Snigdha), Hard to digest (Guru).

SOUR
Improves digestion.
Nourishes the body.
Strengthens sense organs.
Boosts testosterone, Decreases laziness.
Hot (Ushna), Increases weight (Snigdha), Hard to digest (Guru).

SALT
Improves digestion.
Increases water retention in the body.
Piercing (Tikshna): reaches all corners of the body easily.
Excision: Breaks down the food particles.
Hot (Ushna), Increases weight (Snigdha), Hard to digest (Guru).

PUNGENT
Improves digestion.
Decreases water retention in the body and muscle growth.
Decreases sexual interest and performance.
Strengthens sense organs.
Good for skin.
Hot (Ushna), Decreases weight (Rooksha), Easy to digest (Laghu).

ASTRINGENT
Dries moisture from body (Decreases oiliness from the skin).
Decreases sexual interest and performance.
Decreases urine production.
Not good for heart.
Decreases sputum production.
cool (Sheeta), Decreases weight (Rooksha), Easy to digest (Laghu).

BITTER
Cleans the body.
Kills germs inside the stomach.
Good during fever.
Not good for heart.
Decreases sweating and cholesterol.
Decreases sexual interest and performance.
Produces coolness in the body.
Cool (Sheeta), Decreases weight (Rooksha), Easy to digest (Laghu).
