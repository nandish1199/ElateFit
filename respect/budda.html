  Q: Who is Buddha?

  • The world remembers only those greatest teachers who concurred the near impossible task that is controlling the mind completely, and also helped others by their magnificent teachings to concur the mind (Chitta). I bow down to the greatest teacher Buddha.
  • Buddha taught the world that health problems, old age, and death are the realities that no one can get rid of.
  • Buddha given the knowledge to the world that happiness is temporary and sadness is permanent on this earth.
  • Buddha given the knowledge to the world that Salvation(Nirvan) is the ultimate bliss.
  • Four Noble truths taught by Buddha:
    • All beings experience misery during their lifetime.
    • The origin of misery is desire.
    • The cessation of misery can be achieved through cessation of desire and thoughts.
    • To stop misery completely you must follow the path of Nirvan(Equilibrium, The state of thoughtlessness).
  • Buddha given 8 ways to perfect to get the Salvation(Nirvan) that are as follows:
    • Samyak Drusti: Observe the surroundings clearly.
    • Samyak Sankalp: Get out of false restrictions and take correct decisions.
    • Samyak wak: Talk right.
    • Samyak Karmanyata: Do right work, and work persistently.
    • Samyak Ajivika: Earn through right ways.
    • Samyak Vyayam: Practice yoga to keep body and the mind strong.
    • Samyak Smriti: Get rid of promises or conduct that you have been following from the past. Adjust and change according to time.
    • Samyak Samadhi: You will be out of all thoughts you stay in the equilibrium state where neither sadness and happiness can touch you. You will be out of all the sadness and all the thoughts.

GREAT TEACHINGS
Anekajati samsaram sandhavissam anibbisam Gahakarakam bavesanto dukkha jati punapunam Gahakaraka dittho' si puna geham na kahasi Sabba te phasuka bhagga gahakatam visamkhitam Vesamkharagatam cittam tanhanam khayamajjhaga.
Hey Ishwar(House Builder, God) I have taken birth on this earth(Samsar) many times, But now I have understood you, this is my last life(incarnation), You shall never build house for me again in this Samsara(earth). I shall never take birth on this Samsara(Earth) again.
House Builder = Ishwar(God), House = Body.
