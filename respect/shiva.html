  Q: Who is Mahadev?

  • The world remembers only those greatest teachers who concurred the near impossible task that is controlling the mind completely, and also helped others by their magnificent teachings to concur the mind (Chitta). I bow down to the greatest teacher Adiyogi.
  • The entire Universe will destroy, the entire living beings will die and ultimately their existence will become false, but the only Purusha who is unaffected from birth and death is lord Shiva who will stay forever, that's why we call him as the Beautiful Truth that never fades. (Satyam Shivam Sundaram)
  • Shiva is the first yogi who taught us how to take control over our mind completely through yogic practices, Hence we call him as Adiyogi (The first yogi).
  • Shiva is Nirgun, which means his chitta(mind) is neither distracted from good things nor from bad things.
  • Shiva is called as Chidananda, which means he reached the state of Nirvikalpa Samadhi where nothing in this entire universe can make him sad, he always stays in equanimous state.
  • Nirvakalpa Samadhi the last stage of yoga, if a yogi reaches this stage then he realizes that he is Shiva himself, hence, Shiva is everywhere every living being every minute particle in the entire universe is the Shiva's creation.
  • Shiva taught us Hathayoga, Rajayoga the two beautiful ways where a yogi can take control over their mind completely, to reach equilibrium or to attain salvation and to get rid of the birth and death cycle.
  • Hathayoga: Taking control over mind(getting rid of all the thoughts from the mind) through retention of breath(Pranayama).
  • Rajayoga: Taking control over mind(getting rid of all the thoughts from the mind) through gazing(linga yoga or shivayoga).
  • All the praising words from all the languages combined together will be less to praise our lord Shiva.
