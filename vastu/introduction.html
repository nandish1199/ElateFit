VASTU SHASTRA
Vastu Shastra is not about breaking walls and building it in the auspicious direction. Because every direction and every place is auspicious. We all know that God lives everywhere (Sarvatrayami) hence every place every direction is auspicious.
Vastu Shastra is the healthy construction of a house that promotes health and peaceful living.
Vastu Shastra differs from place to place. Example: Construction design near ocean and the construction design on hill areas are totally different, because the winds flow from ocean towards earth all the time hence it is not preferred to attach main doors facing ocean, and on hills climate changes most often and winds flow randomly depends upon the situation, hence it is preferred to attach small windows and small doors to the house.
Vastu Shastra is highly scientific, Vastu Shastra just not talk about the exterior structure of the house, it also gives brilliant knowledge on interiors. Example: In Vastu Shastra it is preferred to build kitchen and the bedroom in the opposite direction because of the noise pollution in the kitchen can disturb the sleep. This is just one example that i am giving you there are hundreds of analysis according to Vastu Shastra.
Vastu Shastra also gives the knowledge on what plants should be planted inside and outside the house. Example: It is not recommended to plant coconut tree near the house because will destroy the foundations of the house, as it has fibrous root, and it is recommended to plant aloe vera plant inside the house.
Vastu Shastra also gives the knowledge on ventilation, window types and size, direction of windows, to get good sunlight inside the house and to get good amount of fresh air. Example: It is recommended to place white cotton curtains to the windows because it will disperse the light in the house and kill most of the bacteria and viruses.
Vastu Shastra also gives the knowledge on safety features inside the house. Example: It is recommended to place water vessels surrounding the stove or near the stove.
Vastu Shastra also gives the knowledge on many other scenarios that cannot be given within one blog.
